    Torso Twist

    Click to see instructions

    How to perform:

    1. Sit tall in your chair.
    2. Place right hand on left knee.
    3. Gently twist torso to the left.
    4. Hold for 5 breaths, then switch.

    Improves Posture

    Shoulder Rolls

    Click to see instructions

    How to perform:

    1. Sit or stand up tall.
    2. Inhale and lift shoulders to your ears.
    3. Exhale and roll them back and down.
    4. Repeat 5 times slowly.

    Releases Stress

    Seated Forward Fold

    Click to see instructions

    How to perform:

    1. Push chair slightly back.
    2. Inhale deeply, reach arms up.
    3. Exhale, fold forward over your legs.
    4. Let head hang heavy for 30s.

    Knowledge & Awareness

    Understanding clinical mental health definitions and strategies.

    Understanding Anxiety

    Anxiety is a normal, predictable emotional response to stressful situations. However, when feelings of intense fear and distress become overwhelming and prevent us from doing everyday activities, it might be an anxiety disorder.

    Read Clinical Definition →

    Depression (Major Depressive Disorder)

    Depression is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act. It causes feelings of sadness and/or a loss of interest in activities you once enjoyed.

    Learn about symptoms →

    Stress Management Techniques

    Chronic stress affects the body neurologically. The most effective non-medical interventions include regulating the nervous system via deep somatic breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, and cognitive behavioral restructuring.
